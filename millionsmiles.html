About Million Smiles

Founded in early 2019 by a young patriotic Pakistan woman Ms. Umme Muhmammd who moved back from New York with a vision to foster the 130 Million Youth o Pakistan by equipping them with basic level of skills to maximize their success in life & encourage to develop their fullest potential in spirit, mind and body. The Co-Founder of Million Smiles Mr. Zeshan Afzal who also left this job in Manhattan, New York while working at the wall street dreaming to make a tengible difference for the 130 Million Youth of Pakistan with special focus on their development. His dream to inculcate core set of values revolving around personal development, experimental learning, travel, patriotism and religious harmony. We believe in global leadership potential of Pakistani youth which is why are shaping possibilities for them. We are empowering the youth of Pakistan by exploring their talent through extensive youth development programs. Million Smiles is an ecosystem that enables Pakistan's patriotic potential to play.
